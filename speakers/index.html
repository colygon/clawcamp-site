Share What You Know

ClawCamp mentors are builders who show up and help others level up. You don't need to be a keynote speaker or an industry expert. If you've built something with AI agents, deployed a model, or figured out a workflow that works — someone at the camp wants to learn from you.

Mentors lead workshops, give demos, sit on panels, or simply pair with attendees during build sessions. You choose the format. We handle the rest.

  • Lead a workshop — teach a skill hands-on, from beginner setup to advanced multi-agent patterns
  • Give a demo — show what you've built and how it works
  • Join a panel — share your perspective on where AI agents are heading
  • Pair with builders — help attendees debug, deploy, or ship during build sessions

Every mentor gets a free VIP pass, recognition on the site, and the chance to connect with the most active AI builder community in the world.
