Curriculum Policy

ClawCamp does not have a single official curriculum. We believe:

  • Anyone can create their own curriculum. Your workshop, your format, your content.
  • We provide example curricula as starting points for common formats (OpenClaw setup, agent building, demo day hosting, etc.)
  • Everyone is asked to share back. When you build something that works, add it to the shared library. The whole network gets better.
